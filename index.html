    Lake-Bunyonyi -3 days

    Full day excursion on Lake Bunyonyi

    On day 2 of 3 days Lake Bunyonyi Tour, have your breakfast and head out for a full day adventure on Lake Bunyonyi starting with a community tour where you will meet the Bakiga and Batwa that reside near the shores of Lake Bunyonyi. You will be able to spend some time with them interacting about their life styles. This is yet the best day to enjoy birding as well as walking adventure. After spending a half day, return to your lodge for lunch after which you will spend your afternoon on a canoe ride exploring different islands since the lake has 29 islands. There is yet an option of swimming only done by experienced swimmers since the lake is very deep. You may also enjoy Zip lining on top of the deepest lake which is yet a fascinating activity. Return to your lodge later in the evening for dinner and overnight sleep..
