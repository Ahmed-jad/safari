Bird Safari Tour -15 days

From: $3330 per person mid-range

Program

  • Day 1: Welcome to Uganda! Airport transfer and sight-seeing in Kampala.
  • Day 2: To Budongo Forest and bird safaris.
  • Day 3: Budongo Forest and Murchison Falls National park.
  • Day 4: Murchison Falls National park.
  • Day 5: Hoima.
  • Day 6: To Fort Portal.
  • Day 7: Semuliki Game Reserve.
  • Day 8: Kibale Forest National Park and Queen Elizabeth National Park.
  • Day 9: Queen Elizabeth National Park.
  • Day 10: Ishasha andBwindi Impenetrable Forest National.Park.
  • Day 11: Gorilla Tracking Bamboo Trail.
  • Day 12: Ruhija, Waterfall Trail.
  • Day 13: Lake Bunyonyi and Echuya Forest.
  • Day 14: Lake MburoNatinalPark.
  • Day 15: Mbamba Bay and Entebbe.

    • Accommodation

    Accommodation is based on standard twin/double rooms (shared) in mid-range hotels.

    Inclusions

  • Transport in a 4wd mini bus with pop-up roof for excellent game viewing (or upgrade, see ‘Transport’).
  • All fuel for the tour.
  • English speaking Ugandan driver/guide.
  • Accommodation as listed in the program
  • Meals according to the program.
  • Park-entrance fees for Queen Elizabeth NP and Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.
  • All activities described in the itinerary.

    • Exclusions

  • International air fares.
  • Entry visa Uganda.
  • Meals and snacks which are not inclusive in the accommodation.
  • A porter for during the gorilla tracking’s (someone who carries your daypack).
  • Personal (medical/travel) insurance.
  • Tips and gratuities to rangers, driver/guide, porter, hotel staff, etc.
  • All expenses of personal nature (e.g. drinks, laundry, optional activities, souvenirs etc.).

    • Meal plan

    The cost of the tour includes some breakfasts, but not all. Lunch and dinner is not included in the costing. Your meals are available at your accommodation. On days when you are travelling, a packed lunch can be provided by you accommodation, but you prefer to eat at a local restaurant instead, please go ahead! All options are at your own cost.
