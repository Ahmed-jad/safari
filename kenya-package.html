8- DAYS - KENYA MASAI MARA SAFARI

Kenya adventurous safari,Travellers admire the magnificent elephants in Amboseli, enjoy the romance of Meru and explore the untamed wildlife in the Masai Mara. This fully inclusive package includes all accommodation, meals, safari activities as well as transfers to and from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.

Journey destinations:

  • Nairobi
  • Amboseli
  • Meru
  • Masai Mara

Day 1 and 8: Nairobi Luxuer Hotel

Day 2 + 3: Amboseli

The spectacular backdrop of Mt Kilimanjaro frames the semi-arid grasslands of Amboseli. Home to one of Africa’s largest elephant populations, Amboseli is a scenic mosaic of swamps with water year-round, therefore, sustaining an incredible variety of birdlife and game. The Tortilis Camp nestles on a hillside from where magnificent views of Mt Kilimanjaro can be admired. En-suite Luxury Tents offer lovely safari accommodation. Located in a private conservancy bordering the National Park, guests can enjoy thrilling walking safaris and stunning African sunsets.

  • Private conservancy bordering Amboseli National Park
  • Excellent views of Mount Kilimanjaro
  • Safari drives in custom-designed safari vehicles
  • Bush walks with qualified and local guides
  • Extravagant bush breakfasts,

Day 4 + 5: Meru National Park

Meru features an arid landscape with permanent and seasonal rivers. The 21,000-acre rhino sanctuary is located within the heart of this park providing a rare opportunity to admire the 'Big 5' in this remote and wild region of Kenya. Elsa's Kopje Meru nestles into a rocky kopje where charming cottage suites are constructed around the natural features of the hill. From each breath-taking vistas over the sweeping Meru plain.

  • Rhino sanctuary in the heart of Meru Park
  • Safari drives in custom-designed safari vehicles
  • Nature walks up Mugwhango Hill with knowledgeable guides

Day 6 + 7: Masai Mara

The Masai Mara, offering one of the best wildlife viewing regions in Africa, features rolling plains, thunderous rivers and acacia thickets. Home to an abundance of migratory and resident animals, guests stay in the Masai Mara at the Sand River Masai Mara, a stylish luxury camp located along the banks of the Sand River. The Luxury Tents are designed to recreate a 1930’s luxury safari.

  • Private and secluded area of Masai Mara
  • Situated on the Northerly migration path
  • Safari drives in custom-designed safari vehicles

Day 8 - Final Day in Nairobi Kenya.
